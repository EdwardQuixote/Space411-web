Kimanzi’s

June 5, 2013 Comemnts (0) Kimanzi's

The Kimanzi’s; Mercy Museo and Faith Mutheu. They went to Ndururu School for their primary education and later joined Maliku Girls School in 2007 for their high school education. After clearing their high school education, they joined Nairobi Aviation College and studied a diploma course in Public Relations.
The first thing you notice when you meet this pair is their model physique; averagely tall, slender and exquisite. Well, they are unique models and more to it is that they are professional singers. Due to their outstanding identity they have featured in a lot of weddings as flower girls. They were so popular in high school due to their talent in singing. Bearing in mind that no everyone has a look-alike they feel nice about being identical twins, it indeed is a blessing from God that they do not take for granted. Twins really do have a tight natural bond of unending companionship. They have never been admitted to separate schools; they’ve been together in all institutions.
The identical gene does run through their family, their maternal grandfather had an identical twin brother too. Apart from their identity cooking, reading novels and swimming are some of the things they have in common. However, in contrast, Faith is more social than Mercy.
Currently they are assisting their mother to run a children’s home in Huruma grounds as part of their charitable and humanitarian project. To them The Identical family is a good platform to showcase their talent. It also gives them a feeling of acceptance as twins.
